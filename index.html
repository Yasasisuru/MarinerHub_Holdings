About Us

Welcome to MarinerHub Holdings, where maritime excellence meets a legacy of craftsmanship. With 30 years of experience, we are proud to be a leading authority in dockyard services, catering to both commercial and private vessel needs. Established in 1993, our dockyard has been a cornerstone of the maritime industry, consistently delivering top-notch solutions for all types of vessels. Over the years, we've built a reputation for excellence, earning the trust of captains, ship owners, and maritime enthusiasts worldwide.
